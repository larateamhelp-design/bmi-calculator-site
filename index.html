BMI calculator FAQ

How do I calculate BMI using kg and cm?

Convert centimeters to meters, then divide kilograms by meters squared. Example: 68 kg and 170 cm is 68 / 1.70² = 23.5.

How do I calculate BMI using pounds and feet?

Convert your height to total inches, divide pounds by inches squared, then multiply by 703.

Is BMI different for women and men?

The adult formula is the same. The interpretation may differ depending on body composition, pregnancy, age, and health history.

Does age affect BMI results?

For adults 20+, standard BMI categories are commonly used. For children and teens, BMI should be interpreted with age- and sex-specific percentiles.

What is a healthy BMI range?

For most adults, 18.5 to 24.9 is considered healthy, 25 to 29.9 is overweight, and 30 or above is obesity.
