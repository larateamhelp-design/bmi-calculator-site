Quick FAQ

How is BMI calculated?

Metric: kg ÷ meters². Imperial: lbs ÷ inches² × 703.

Does BMI change for women or men?

The adult formula is the same. Body composition still matters.

What is a healthy BMI?

For most adults, 18.5 to 24.9 is considered healthy.

What is a healthy BMI for women?

For most adult women, 18.5 to 24.9 is generally considered a healthy BMI. BMI does not account for pregnancy, muscle mass, or individual health factors.

What is a healthy BMI for men?

For most adult men, 18.5 to 24.9 is generally considered a healthy BMI. Athletic build and muscle mass can make BMI less precise.

Is BMI accurate for athletes?

BMI can be less accurate for athletes because it does not distinguish muscle from fat. A muscular person may have a high BMI without having excess body fat.

What is a BMI of 30?

A BMI of 30 is the starting point for the obesity category for most adults. It is a screening measure, not a diagnosis by itself.

How can I lower my BMI?

Lowering BMI usually involves gradual weight loss through nutrition, physical activity, sleep, and sustainable habits. Talk with a healthcare professional for personal guidance.
